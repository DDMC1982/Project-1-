Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know. Can't find your answer? Call us on 07775 706743.

Do you cover the whole Isle of Wight?
Yes — from our base in Newport (PO30) we cover the entire Isle of Wight including Ryde, Cowes, Sandown, Shanklin, Ventnor, Freshwater, Yarmouth, Bembridge and all surrounding villages and rural areas.
Are you Gas Safe registered?
Yes. A D Smith Plumbing and Heating is fully Gas Safe registered. It is a legal requirement in the UK that all gas work — on boilers, cookers and fires — is carried out by a Gas Safe registered engineer.
Do you charge a call-out fee?
No. We do not charge a call-out fee. We provide free, no-obligation quotations on all jobs — whether it's a minor repair or a full central heating installation.
Do you provide landlord gas safety certificates (CP12)?
Yes. We issue CP12 landlord gas safety certificates — a legal requirement for all rental properties in the UK. We carry out the full annual inspection and provide the certificate on the same visit.
How long have you been trading?
A D Smith Plumbing and Heating has been serving Isle of Wight homes since 1988 — over 35 years. We are a small, independent local business with a strong reputation for honest, reliable work at fair prices, approved by Isle of Wight Trading Standards.
Are you approved by Isle of Wight Trading Standards?
Yes — we are approved by Isle of Wight Trading Standards, meaning we have been independently assessed for quality workmanship, reliability and fair trading practices. This is your independent assurance that we operate to a high standard.
